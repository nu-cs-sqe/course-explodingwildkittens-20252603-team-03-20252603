- Draw a card at the end of your turn
- If you draw an Exploding Kitten, you explode and are out — unless you play a Defuse card 
- Skip — end your turn without drawing
- Attack — end your turn, force next player to take 2 turns
- See the Future — peek at the top 3 cards
- Shuffle — shuffle the deck
- Nope — cancel any action (even another Nope)
- Cat cards — play pairs/triplets to steal cards from other players
- Last player alive wins
